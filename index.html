Why This Truck Matters

The image shown is a picture of my truck before I stopped daily driving it. It is now parked to be my project truck that I work on on the weekends and breaks during school. This truck has been through it all with me and it’s going to get the revamp it deserves.

A big reason I picked this image of my truck was because it means the world to me. It’s not just some beat up pickup to me — it was my first truck and my dream truck rolled into one.

Build Progress

This is a picture of my truck when I had just got done working on it, and I thought it looked better than usual that day.

What I’ve Done So Far

  • Took it apart and painted the axles
  • Sandblasted and powdercoated the frame
  • Bought a wood flatbed
  • Got a diesel engine block that I need to build

What I Plan to Do

  • Install a manual transmission
  • Add smoke stacks in the bed
  • Upgrade the suspension
  • Upgrade the interior
  • Make it fully mechanical (no CPU, ECM, or TCM)

Timeline

  1. I parked it permanently around last February. Since then, I’ve made solid progress with the time I’ve had available.
  2. I hope to have it running by the end of summer, even if it’s not roadworthy yet.
